# Group Discussion: Where Are You on the Spectrum?

iGEN Developer AI Training · Session 1 (Hour 1)

---

Use the last 10–15 minutes of Hour 1 for this discussion. Go around the room once on one prompt, then open up. The goal is to surface real experience — there are no wrong answers.

---

## Discussion Prompts

**1. Where does your current AI use sit?**

Think of a scale from *autocomplete* (AI fills in a line while you type) to *agentic* (AI plans and executes a multi-step task while you review the result). Where does most of your day-to-day AI use fall on that scale right now — and is that where you want it to be?

**2. A time it worked, and a time it didn't.**

Describe the last time AI produced something genuinely useful — something you were surprised by. Then describe the last time it confidently produced something wrong. What was the difference between those two situations?

**3. The task you still wouldn't hand off.**

What's one thing in your current work that you would *not* feel comfortable asking an AI to do — even with supervision? Why? Is it a trust issue, a context issue, or something else?

**4. When did you last slow down to verify?**

Think of a recent moment where you stopped and double-checked AI output rather than accepting it. What triggered the check? What did you find?

**5. What would have to change for you to hand off more?**

If you're not handing the AI complex tasks yet, what's standing in the way? Is it confidence in the tool, clarity of the task, access, team norms, something else?

---

*Facilitator note: Prompt 1 frames the room. Prompts 2 and 4 get at trust calibration, which is the core of Hour 1. Prompts 3 and 5 can be left for time or used to close. No need to cover all five.*
