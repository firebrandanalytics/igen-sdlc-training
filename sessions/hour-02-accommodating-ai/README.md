# Hour 2 — Accommodating the AI: Why Prep Work Is the Work

Still conceptual. The reframe: stop asking "how does AI do what I do?" and
start asking "how do I structure my environment so the AI is at its best?" We
name the AI weaknesses you design around and the two operating principles —
"Documentation is code now" and "Make Correctness Easy." The hour includes one
live demo and one hands-on exercise.

## In this folder

- `vague-ask-exercise.md` — rewrite a vague stakeholder ask into a structured,
  AI-ready spec.
- `C7-ai-ready-spec-template.md` — the spec template you fill in for the exercise.
- `C8-make-correctness-easy-checklist.md` — patterns that take correctness work
  off the AI. A reference for the rest of the course.

## What we're doing this hour

- The reframe: accommodate the AI, don't expect it to accommodate you
- AI weaknesses you design around: no human intuition, context compaction,
  confidently-wrong output
- The economic flip — docs and tooling are now cheap to make, heavily consumed
- Exercise: rewrite a vague ask as an AI-ready spec (`vague-ask-exercise.md`)
